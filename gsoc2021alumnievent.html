Rocket.Chat & Google Summer of Code

Rocket.Chat is delighted to participate as a mentoring organization for the 2021 Google Summer of Code (GSoC) program.

Student application for GSoC 2021 will begin March 29, 2021. In the meantime, connect with Rocket.Chat's GSoC Alumni and Mentors.

Schedule 2021

* All times are BST (London Time)

  • March 29, 2021
  • Sing Li @sing.li
    11:45am - Welcome Students to Rocket.Chat's GSoC 2021!
  • Mrinal Dhar @mrinal.dhar
    12pm - Improve implementation of End-to-End encryption with Signal protocol (GSoC 2017)
  • Adarsh Naidu @adarsh.naidu
    12:30pm - Rocket.Chat Alexa Skill and Google Action Improvements (GSoC 2020)
  • Fongang Rodrique @Fongang.Rodrique
    1:00pm - Interactive APIs documentation (GSoC 2019)
  • Ashwani Yadav @ashwani.yadav
    1:30pm - Omnichannel: Add screen sharing feature in the Livechat widget (GSoC 2020)
  • Amish Mittal @Amish.Mittal
    2:00pm - Implementation of Newsfeed (a social feature) in Rocket.Chat Server (GSoC 2019)
  • Taimur Azhar @taimur.azhar
    2:30pm - Download Manager on Desktop App (GSoC 2020)
  • José Renan @joserenan
    3:00pm - Rocket.Chat SDK for Javascript Clients (GSoC 2018)
  • Nick Vidal @nick.vidal
    3:00pm - Closing Session
  • March 29 - April 13, 2021
  • GSoC @gsoc2021
    Student Application Period: Students can register and submit their applications to mentor organizations. All proposals must be submitted by April 13, 2021.
  • May 17, 2021
  • GSoC @gsoc2021
    Student Projects Announced: Accepted students are paired with a mentor and start planning their projects and milestones.
  • May 17, 2021 - June 7, 2021
  • GSoC @gsoc2021
    Community Bonding: Students spend a month learning more about their organization’s community.
  • June 7, 2021
  • GSoC @gsoc2021
    Coding Begins: Students work on their Google Summer of Code projects.
  • July 12 - 16, 2021
  • GSoC @gsoc2021
    Evaluations: Mentors and students submit their evaluations of one another.
  • August 16 - 23, 2021
  • GSoC @gsoc2021
    Students Submit Code and Final Evaluations: Students submit their code, project summaries, and final evaluations of their mentors.
  • August 23 - 30, 2021
  • GSoC @gsoc2021
    Mentors Submit Final Evaluations: Mentors review student code samples and determine if the students have successfully completed their Google Summer of Code 2021 project.
  • August 31, 2021
  • GSoC @gsoc2021
    Results Announced: Students are notified of the pass/fail status of their Google Summer of Code 2021 projects.
